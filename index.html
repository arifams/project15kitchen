Project Kitchen

Cook. Learn. Build. Share.

A journey to 15 different kitchen.

What?

Project 15 kitchen is my journey to learn and find out how my friends cook, what do they eat, but also what we can do to make better food to eat.

Why

To share good food, good laugh and good fun time with friends and family. Also to build healthy lifestyle awareness and learn how to cook from each other.

How?

Travel to 15 kitchen then build apps to share our tips to get delicious, healthy, affordable local food and the recipe collected during this journey.


A LITTLE ABOUT OUR ARIF

My name is Arif and I do believe life is beautiful. Some of my friends and family called me with many different names but it doesn't matter. The most important thing is; I do have friends and family, they are really awesome human being. On May 2016 I finished my study as a chef. During my study there were a lot of awesome people keep encouraging me to move forward and walk this path. Nobody said it was an easy walk but nobody also said it was an impossible task. So here I am, create my own happines with a journey to 15 friends and family kitchen. To cook. To learn. To share. To build. To thankful. I don't know what it will bring me but I won't worry. As I believe that the intention is pure so is our soul, our heart, our cups and our walk will lead us to a beautiful journey.

I do developing apps sometime but once upon a time a was so ill and I cannot build any apps at that time. After long and winding road to find the cure I found out that actually eat proper food does really help to get back to my own health. What we consume really does take a huge part of our life (or at least my life). So while developing mobile apps for recipe collected during this journey, I'm gonna learn from my host as I know they also have their own wisdom and knowledge about what do they eat and treat their food at their kitchen. I hope we can share or perhaps create together a better life through the food that we consume. If you would like to know, contact me here for the update. Bear with me, my friends :)

Laia’s Amanida de Arroz (Laia’s Rice Salad)

This is Catalonia traditional Laia's family recipe. Boil rice and dress it with blended anchovies and olive oil. Then add olives, surimi, corn and that was all! You can add slice pinneaple if you would like to :)

Visit Recipe Video

Sangria Catalonia

There has to be the same part of wine than the other liquids, so for 10l sangria: 5l red wine 2l Catalonia Champagne (Cava) 1,5l orange soda 1,5l lemon soda You can add extra 1l pineapple juice and 1l apple juice for more fruity flavour. First add pieces of lemon, orange and apple to the wine and let it macerate for 5 hours at least. Then add the rest and serve it so cool with ice! 😊

A Love Story

While we are cooking in their kitchen in Montmello, Jordi told me that once he has a similar dream like mine. "You know, I also graduated from cooking school and would like to go travel around the world to cook"

So when I asked what stop him doing it, Laia, his daughter, enter the room. He was laughing when he said "I became a dad". Pure based on curiosity I asked him again, "Do you ever regret that decision". He was smiling and said "Everyone has their own path. This is my path and I never regret it"

His wife Dolors came to the room. She helped him to cook. While they are cooking, definitely I could see that he is a happy man.

Published July 28th 2016.
